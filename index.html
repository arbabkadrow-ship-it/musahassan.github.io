Elevator Pitch

My professional story in cybersecurity — recorded and ready.

Pitch Script

Hi, my name is Musa Hassan, and I am an aspiring Junior Cybersecurity Analyst, currently training through the ALX Cybersecurity track. I balance office work with intensive study, and over the past months I’ve built a strong foundation in network security, cyber threats, and security best practices while also working on tech projects and my own portfolio website.

My top skills are problem‑solving, clear communication, and attention to detail. I’ve applied these by working with a team to design an AI‑powered advertising assistant for small e‑commerce businesses, creating wireframes in Figma, and gathering user feedback to improve our solution. Guided by the GCGO “Solve Real Problems with Tech,” I focus on using technology to protect people, systems, and data and to make real challenges easier to manage.

What makes me stand out is my ability to combine a security mindset with empathy for users and a disciplined personal routine that keeps me focused and resilient. I’m excited to contribute as a Junior Cybersecurity Analyst by helping organizations strengthen their defenses, respond effectively to issues, and continuously improve their security posture. I’d be delighted to bring my skills, curiosity, and commitment to your team and help you protect what matters most.
